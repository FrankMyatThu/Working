TokenService.Prerequisite
==========================

get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.Owin
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.Owin.Cors
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.Owin.Security.jwt
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.AspNet.WebApi.Cors
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.AspNet.WebApi.Owin
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.Aspnet.Identity.Owin
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-package Microsoft.Aspnet.Identity.EntityFramework

/// for startup.cs
get-project <<ProjectName>> | install-Package Microsoft.Owin.Host.SystemWeb

/// to update all api(s)
Update-Package -ProjectName <<ProjectName>>
